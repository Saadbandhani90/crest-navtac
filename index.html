What Makes Us No.1 In Innovation

The question asked in CREST Olympiads focuses on the practical knowledge of the student rather tha theorotical knowledge. CREST Olympiads are highly competitive and the major competitive exams acrod the world are going to be online the coming years,therefore CREST Olympiads prove to be foundations of preparation of students for exams like JEE,NEE,etc.

What's there for Special Needs Students?

Asia's 1st online platform to conduct Olympiad exams for special needs students. CREST Olympiads has made suitable modifications in the curriculum & examination system to meet the needs of such students & helping them to give an exposure of competitive environment that exists. Students from Prep/Kg till grade 10 can appear.
